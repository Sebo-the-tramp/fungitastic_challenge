\section{Conclusion}

The paper is now aligned with the broader README-defined story: teaser comparisons for segmentation, a backbone-family ablation, and a classifier ablation. At the current repo state, the completed evidence still comes from the first DINOv3 slice, where \texttt{cls+masked} is the strongest MLP representation at both 224 and 448, and the best 224 $k$-NN result follows the same ordering. The main message is therefore stable but intentionally local: mask-aware features help most when fused with global context. The remaining work is to populate that story across the full DINOv3 suite, the 2000-class SAM-3 setting, the broader backbone family set, and the full classifier comparison.
